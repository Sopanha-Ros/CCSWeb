Frequently Asked Questions

What types of embedded boards and sensors do you sell?

We offer a wide range of embedded boards and sensors, including Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and various environmental sensors. These products are ideal for educational projects and help enhance STEM learning.

Are your products suitable for beginners and educators?

Yes! Our embedded boards, sensors, and coding robots are designed for all skill levels. We provide resources, tutorials, and customer support to help both beginners and educators successfully implement these technologies in their projects.

Do you offer guidance on integrating your products into educational curriculums?

Absolutely! We provide guidance and resources to help educators integrate our products into their curricula, ensuring students can benefit from hands-on learning experiences.

How do your STEM coding robots enhance learning experiences in educational settings?

Our STEM coding robots offer interactive and engaging learning experiences that promote creativity and critical thinking. By allowing students to code and control robots, they gain practical skills in programming, robotics, and engineering principles, which are essential for their future careers.

What types of support do you provide for troubleshooting and project development?

We offer a variety of support options, including a dedicated help center, online resources, and expert assistance via email or chat. Whether you encounter technical issues or need advice on project development, our team is here to guide you through the process, ensuring you maximize the potential of our products.
